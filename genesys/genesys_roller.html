Using xd format where x is the number of dice and d is the die type.

Dice Types:
  • a, Ability die.
  • b, Bonus die.
  • y, Proficiency die.
  • s, Setback die.
  • d, Difficulty.
  • c, Challenge die.
  • f, Special die.

Example, 2 ability dice & 1 proficiency die verse a 2 difficulty: 2a 1y 2d
